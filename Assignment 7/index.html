Complex Equations

Equation 1:

Quadratic Formula: \(x = \frac{{-b \pm \sqrt{{b^2 - 4ac}}}}{{2a}}\)

Equation 2:

Sum of First \(n\) Natural Numbers: \(S = \frac{{n(n+1)}}{2}\)

Equation 3:

Pythagorean Theorem: \(c^2 = a^2 + b^2\)

Equation 4:

Binomial Theorem: \((a+b)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} {n \choose k} a^{n-k} b^k\)

Equation 5:

Trigonometric Identity: \(\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1\)

Equation 6:

Derivative of \(e^x\): \(\frac{{d}}{{dx}} (e^x) = e^x\)

Equation 7:

Limit Definition of Derivative: \(f'(x) = \lim_{{h \to 0}} \frac{{f(x+h) - f(x)}}{h}\)

Equation 8:

Fourier Transform: \(F(\omega) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(t) e^{-i\omega t} dt\)

Equation 9:

Gaussian Integral: \(\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}\)

Equation 10:

Quaternion Formula: \(q = a + bi + cj + dk\)
